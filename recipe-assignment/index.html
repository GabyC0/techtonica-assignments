Fast Spanish Rice

I came up with this recipe one day that I was craving my mother's rice but couldn't make it because I was limited on time...and ingredients. Fair warning: it's a little spicy!

Ingredients:

  • Long grain rice
  • Oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Chicken bullon
  • Canned "Pato"/tomato sauce(of your choice)
  • Optional: canned corn kernels

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil(any oil to fry) in a pan or pot over medium heat.
  2. When oil is hot, add 1 cup of rice to the oil and stir until brown (careful not to burn it!).
  3. Add two cups of water and stir.
  4. Stir in the small can of "Pato"/tomato sauce.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of garlic powder.
  6. Add 1 tablespoon of chicken bullon.
  7. Optional: Add canned corn kernels.
  8. Stir everything together and bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce heat to "low" and cover for 20 minutes or until all water is absorbed and you can no longer see it bubbling at the top.
